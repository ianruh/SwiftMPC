  • SymbolicObjective

    A symbolic objectibe that can be passed to a solver to be minimized.

    Due to representing almost everything symbolically internally, it isn’t the most performant way of represnting a problem. However, it can also generate a ‘numeric’ objective that can then be compiled and passed to a solver. Since the compiler can perform optimizations on the generated code, numeric objectives are much faster and should be used for anything requiring realtime operation, except for the simplest of toy problems.
